The cards below show dates on which sessions have been automatically scheduled. Click on a date card to see all of the scheduled sessions for that date.

You should see 1 working week of dates in the future, and 1 working work of dates in the past. You can use the search bar and date picker to display all entries for a past or current calendar month.

Date cards appear in the ‘To Be Completed’ column if 1 or more individual sessions on that date are awaiting submission. Dates move to the ‘Completed’ column as soon as all sessions on that date are submitted.
